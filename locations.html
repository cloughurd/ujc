Home to the popular obstacle Wayne's World, Five Mile is a popular wheeling spot for offroaders near and far. The hills and trails all around offer a great variety in offroad opportunity.
The old train tunnels up Elberta Slant road are some of the most requested offroad destinations for it's uniqueness. A network of trails in the surrounding area offer endless fun.
A popular spot, Little Moab features some fairly intense obstacles. Just a short drive from most in the Utah County area, Little Moab is a common destination for those wanting to test their rigs. Often referred to as more of an offroading playground than a trail.
AF Canyon is a very easily accessed location that offers tons of offroad adventure. It can be very busy during the summer, and is not open currently. Even if you keep asking, sadly. Features the popular forest lake trail.
